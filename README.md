📱 LifeCard: Patient App
The LifeCard Patient App is the personal health companion for the LifeCard ecosystem. It empowers users to manage their digital health identity, secure physical hardware, and connect with a community of survivors.

🌟 Key Features
1. Digital Health Identity
Instant ID: View and download your personalized Medical QR code for quick scanning by healthcare providers.

Hardware Ordering: Direct integration to order Physical LifeCards or NFC/RFID Wearables (bracelets/tags) directly through the app.

2. Survival Hub (Community & Mental Health)
Shared Journeys: A dedicated space to read and post "Survival Stories."

Peer Support: Connect with others who have overcome similar medical challenges to foster resilience and combat depression through community support.

🔐 Security & Access Protocols
Device Integrity (Single Session Policy)
To protect sensitive personal health information (PHI), the app enforces strict session management:

Single Sign-In: To prevent unauthorized remote access and credential sharing, only one active session is allowed per account at any time.

Mandatory Logout: Users must explicitly sign out to release the session for use on another device.

Admin Reset: If a session remains active on a lost or inaccessible device, the user must contact a System Administrator to manually terminate the session from the Central Command.
